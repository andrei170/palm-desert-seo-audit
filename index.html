Why a great site can still be invisible to AI

Traditional SEO and AI-search visibility are different games. Your site is built for humans (beautifully) and for Google's classic index. But AI assistants build their answers from structured, machine-readable trust signals: schema markup, named credentials, dense third-party citations, and clean question-and-answer content. Those are exactly the layers your site is light on. The reputation is real; it is just not in a form the AI can quote.

The 5 fixes that close the gap

Highest impact

1Surface and structure the credentials

Lead with the Certified Specialist designation (if held), bar admission, JD/BA, years in practice and affiliations, then encode them in Attorney + LegalService schema. This is the single change most likely to move you into the named shortlist.

High impact

2Add direct-answer blocks for the money questions

Clean, quotable answers to "Who is the best estate planning attorney in Palm Desert?", "Trust vs. will in California?", "How much does a living trust cost in the Coachella Valley?" These self-contained snippets are what AI Overviews and Perplexity actually cite.

Authority

3Build out third-party citations

Get densely and consistently listed on Justia, Super Lawyers, Avvo, Best Lawyers, NAEPC and local Coachella Valley press. AI engines triangulate trust from these; your competitors are heavily present and you are thin.

Differentiation

4Own the niches competitors ignore

Your bilingual team, blended-family expertise and business-owner focus are genuine differentiators. Built into dedicated, structured pages ("estate planning for business owners in Palm Desert," "Spanish-speaking estate attorney Coachella Valley"), they become categories you can own outright in AI answers.

Measurement

5Track AI citations over time

We monitor ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews and Perplexity for your core terms so you can watch Palm Desert Law move from "not mentioned" to "cited and recommended."

Bottom line

Palm Desert Law does not need a redesign. You need the credential and citation layer that turns an excellent brand into the firm an AI assistant recommends by name. That is a focused project: structure the credentials, add answer content, build the off-site citations, and measure the lift. It puts you in front of desert families at the exact moment they are choosing who to trust with their legacy.
